Story of Thalia's

Our business began in downtown Tempe, close enough to ASU campus that students are our most frequent customers. We opened our doors in 2015, and have slowly grown from a family-only business ever since then. We have a comfortable environment that is welcoming. We develop our own espresso and tea drink recipes with your health in mind, focusing on interesting and high quality ingredients that enhance the coffee and tea. We have a build-your-own trail mix station to meet your snacking needs instead of the usual high-sugar pastries. We bring the value of a comfortable spot and our proximity to ASU campus makes it easy to make our space a reprieve before or after class. We are open late until 10pm to accommodate students’ busy homework schedules. People can come here easily to have meetings or study individually; we work hard to accommodate them all.
